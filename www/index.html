Start Painting


Koala is a fun drawing app that lets you and your friends paint together! To get started, select or add a new canvas.
Tap the plus button to select your painting tools. You can change the tool's color, width, and opacity, and see the preview before painting!
If you want your friends to join, tap the envelope button.
To hide the toolbar, tap the the minus button.
Enjoy!
